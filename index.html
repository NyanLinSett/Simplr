Our Motto:
"Live Simpler"
Here, at Simplr, we strive to design and cultivate a digital community of minimalists and to spread awareness about the importance of simple living. Our website is the gateway to the realm of simpler yet fulfilling lives.
Why Minimalism?

Our world suffers from abundance crisis- we possess more than that is necessary, yet we are hungry for more and tend to forget to be grateful. The message of minimalism helps us to take a turn and change our lives, to be able to live as we actually desire.
